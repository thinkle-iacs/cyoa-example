Hello, Llama

Though it feels rather strange to be talking to a Llama, you decide to brave it and say hello.

Stepping off your bike so as not to frighten it, you take a few steps forward and, when the Llama makes eye contact with you, you say, "Hello, Llama."

The Llama blinks at you, then says, "Hello, human. I am Luna, the talking Llama. I am pleased to meet you."
